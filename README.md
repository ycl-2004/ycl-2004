### 🌱 Who I am · 我是谁

I'm **Yi-Chen Lin (YC)** — an Electrical Engineering student at **UBC**, living between **Vancouver and Taiwan**. I'm the kind of person where **理性 and 浪漫 live side by side**: I use engineering thinking to make things solid, and I keep the romance in the details. I build, I make music, I read, I write, and I'm always learning something new.

What I care about most: making work that feels **warm, human, and "not like AI"** — even when AI is doing half the work. Ideas are easy; **building** is where the magic happens.

我是 YC，一个在 AI 时代**认真生活、浪漫创作**的人。UBC 电子工程在读，常驻温哥华／台湾。理性和浪漫共存——用工程思维把事情做扎实，把浪漫留在细节里。喜欢 build 东西、做音乐、读书、记录生活，一直在成长。比起一路完美，我更在意路上**永远在认真发生**。
